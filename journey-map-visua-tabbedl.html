Service Blueprint — Grain Pricing Tool
The tool closes the gap between merchant decisions and originator execution — in real time. The ML model provides a starting point: competitive territory, suggested pricing, targeted leads. But the merchant's market knowledge, forward judgment, and relationships are what drive the strategy. The model is backward-looking. The merchant is forward-looking. The tool works for the merchant. Not the other way around.

Build
Select contract month(s), commodity & date
Merchant selects one or more contract months to build a deferred book. Each month becomes its own scenario with its own map, pricing, and lead list — all with a view to hitting coverage targets.
Deferred book⬡ Pricing Tool
Configure
Enter variables
Merchant inputs posted bid, min/max bid, leeway, and delivery cost — derived from purchased freight futures for that contract month. ML autopopulates suggested min, max, and leeway as a starting point based on market conditions and competitor landscape. Merchant adjusts as needed.
ML-suggested inputs⬡ Pricing Tool · ML
Analyze
View competitive heat map
Merchant views a competitiveness surface overlaid on the map — showing where the elevator can win business based on competitor locations, competitor functions, and the selected contract month. Informs where to focus the selection.
Competitive intelligence⬡ Pricing Tool · ML
Select
Lasso producers, apply filters
Merchant lassos a geographic area on the map to make an initial selection. Applies broad filters to remove categories (recent spot price, recently contacted). Removes individual producers by hand as a final pass.
Spatial selection⬡ Pricing Tool
Model
Nudge inputs, shape updates
Merchant nudges forward pricing inputs and ML updates territory and bid prices for each contract month in real time. No apply button needed.
Real-time modeling⬡ Pricing Tool · ML
Activate
Activate scenario
Merchant activates the scenario — tool automatically cascades forward bid prices to originator lead lists, Salesforce producer accounts, and contract month pricing.
Auto cascade⬡ Pricing Tool · Salesforce
Monitor
Monitor on dashboard
Merchant monitors performance on the dashboard. Contracts are attributed to the scenario automatically. Rejected bids accumulate in real time — merchant can see underperformance without waiting for a report.
Auto attribution⬡ Pricing Tool · ERP
Revise
Revise Scenario → Update Originator Books
Merchant senses a market shift and hits Revise Scenario. Tool pulls the current futures price — ML recalculates suggested bid, min/max, leeway, and freight. Competitive landscape, producer data, and competitor positions all refresh. Merchant reviews, adjusts based on market knowledge the model doesn't have, selects producers, and hits Update Originator Books. Originators are notified immediately.
Merchant judgment + live data⬡ Pricing Tool · ML
Close
Export performance, update position
Merchant exports scenario performance data and manually incorporates closed contracts into position calculations for the following week. Tool boundary — position management still lives outside the system.
Manual reconciliation⬡ Pricing Tool · Excel
