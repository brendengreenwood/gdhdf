Core Problem
Pricing lives in merchants' heads. Originators execute without targeted leads or real-time pricing context. The feedback loop takes a full week.
MVP Unlock
A live scenario connects merchant pricing decisions directly to originator workflows and surfaces outcomes back to the merchant in real time.
Strategic Value
Every outcome — accepted, declined, reason — feeds the ML model. The tool gets smarter with every scenario run.
